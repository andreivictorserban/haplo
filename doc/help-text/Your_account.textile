h1. Your account

To get to your account page click on your name in the top right. 

Your account page lists different options specific to you. 

* Checked out files: view the files you currently have checked out.

* Tasks: view current and future tasks assigned to you.

* View latest updates: view new additions to PRODUCT_NAME matching your interests.

* Latest update topics: select the topics to be included in your latest updates.

* Your account: information about your account including your log in email address, your group memberships, and contact details for your system administrators.

* Change password: click here to change your password.

* Logout: click here to finish using PRODUCT_NAME, and ensure that anyone else using this computer cannot access your information.
